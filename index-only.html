About Bestride

Equip for the best, from your first lesson to your driving test.

Bestride Driving School trains learners to handle real Kenyan roads — roundabouts, junctions, hills, and traffic — with calm, confident control. Every lesson is built around safety first, clear instruction, and steady progress, not shortcuts.

Our instructors are KENHA/NTSA-compliant and patient with first-time drivers, while our fleet of dual-control training vehicles, clearly marked "Driver Under Instruction," keeps you and other road users safe while you learn.

Certified Instructors

Experienced, patient, NTSA-recognised trainers.

Modern Fleet

Dual-control sedans & wagons, well maintained.

Safety First
